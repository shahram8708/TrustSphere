Invisible Protection. Proportionate Response.

TrustSphere evaluates identity trust continuously, intervening only when genuine risk is detected.

1

Silent Monitoring

Device fingerprinting and behavioural biometrics collect signals passively. No interruption to the user experience.

2

Multi Signal Analysis

The Continuous Risk Engine correlates device, behavioural, network, and transaction signals in under 100ms.

3

Composite Risk Scoring

A normalised risk score from 0 to 100 is produced for every session event and updated throughout the session.

Proportionate Response
