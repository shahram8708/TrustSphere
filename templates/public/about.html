Protecting Banking. Respecting Privacy.

TrustSphere is built on the belief that digital banking security should be continuous, contextual, and respectful of the customer. Identity trust should be evaluated from risk signals that are minimised, protected, and explainable. Security interventions should happen only when the evidence justifies them.

Privacy by Design

TrustSphere stores hashed identifiers and anonymised behavioural vectors rather than raw sensitive content. Each signal is collected for a defined risk purpose.

Proportional Response

Trusted activity can proceed quietly while unusual activity receives only the verification level needed. This keeps protection aligned with user experience.

Context Aware Intelligence

Risk decisions are based on device, network, behaviour, session, and policy context. Analysts receive explainable evidence for every significant decision.

The Problem We Solve

Banking identity risk now moves across login, onboarding, recovery, employee systems, and live sessions. TrustSphere connects those contexts into one continuous trust framework.

Account takeover through credential theft, phishing, and SIM swap.
KYC onboarding fraud using synthetic identities and manipulated documents.
Insider privilege misuse through excessive access or export behaviour.
Session hijacking after a legitimate login succeeds.
Device and channel compromise from rooted devices, emulators, and unsafe networks.

The Gap

The gap is not the absence of security tools. The gap is the absence of a privacy preserving trust layer that connects identity, behaviour, device intelligence, access context, and regulatory evidence across the full banking lifecycle.
